Day seven. Keks gave me a task

Keks came today and gave me a slightly unusual task. One kitty settled next to him, and Keks does not know how to write songs at all. So he asked me to find some great piece so he could perform it for her.

As they say, you can do anyone for the one you love ( folk wisdom ).

Found a lyric piece for Cupcake:

Meow meow meow
mrrr meow meow
meow meow
meow

Collection of works of M. Kotidze.
