What have I learned! The Keks training program for beginner kittens has a new master class - "How not to fall through the snow". I was instructed to typeset a small teaching material.

Surface pressure is calculated using the following formula: p = m * g / S.

Given: p 1 = 1635Pa

Calculate: p 2 = m 2 * g / S = 9kg * 9.81N / 0.03m 2 = 2942Pa

where:
p1 - ​​foot pressure on the snow before the New Year holidays,
p2 - foot pressure after the holidays,
m2 - the mass of the cat after the holidays,
S - snow surface area,
g - acceleration of gravity.

The maximum pressure that the first crust can withstand is 2000 Pa.
